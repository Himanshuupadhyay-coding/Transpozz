About Transpozz

Founded in 2023 with a vision to revolutionize the transportation industry in India through technological innovation, Transpozz stands as a leading B2B/B2C platform dedicated to connecting transport companies with shippers, fostering seamless and efficient logistics solutions across the nation. At Transpozz,, we recognize and address the inherent challenges faced by the transport industry in India. Our mission is to transform traditional logistics by leveraging cutting-edge technology,market driven data providing a comprehensive and cost-effective business model tailored for the unique needs of the transportation sector.

What We Actually Do

Our platform acts as a catalyst, facilitating transparency, accessibility, and affordability to streamline logistics operations for a vast network of transport companies and shippers. With a commitment to being a trusted partner, we empower small and medium businesses with technological advancements, financial inclusivity, and supply chain capabilities.
